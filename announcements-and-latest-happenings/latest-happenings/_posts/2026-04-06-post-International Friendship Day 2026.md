<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">The theme for International Friendship Day, <i>Forging Friendships in a Complex World</i>, encourages students to take initiative in building meaningful connections and to appreciate diverse cultures and perspectives. Through these experiences, students develop the understanding and skills needed to contribute to a cohesive and globally connected society.
<br><br>
In line with this theme, our school organised a range of activities that provided students with engaging and meaningful opportunities to deepen their understanding of Singapore’s connections with other countries and to strengthen friendships within the school community.</div>
