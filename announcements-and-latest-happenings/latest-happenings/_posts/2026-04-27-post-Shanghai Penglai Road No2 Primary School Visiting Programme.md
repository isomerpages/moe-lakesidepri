<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">On 27 and 28 April, students and teachers from Shanghai Penglai Primary School visited our school to strengthen friendship and promote student exchange. A key focus of the visit was their “Little Young Writers Programme”, which showcases students’ creativity and achievements in writing.
<br><br>
On 27 April, a highlight was the Little Young Writers Book Showcase, where student authors walked the stage and introduced their newly published books. During recess, a Little Young Writers Meet-and-Greet was held, where the visiting students displayed their works, interacted with our students, and presented selected publications as gifts.
<br><br>
The activities created meaningful opportunities for students to connect through language and creativity, fostering a deeper appreciation for writing, cultural exchange, and peer learning.</div>
