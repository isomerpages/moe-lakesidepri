<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">At Lakeside Primary School, our students were proud to be part of the Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) 60: The ArtsXPerience – Inspire, Connect, Empower.
<br><br>
This vibrant platform provided meaningful opportunities for students to showcase their talents, learn from one another, and grow through the arts, alongside thousands of peers across Singapore.
<br><br>
Through their SYF journey, students developed:
<ul><li>Artistic skills and confidence through performance</li>
<li>Character and values (LPRIDE), such as perseverance and teamwork</li>
<li>Social-emotional and 21st century competencies like communication and collaboration</li></ul>
