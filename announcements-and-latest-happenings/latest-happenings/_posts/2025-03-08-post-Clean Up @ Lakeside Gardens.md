<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">Clean Up @ Lakeside Gardenson 8th March unites Green Club, Community Leadership Club, Innovators’ Club, Parents, Students, Staff, and PacificLight Pte Ltd representatives for environmental action at Jurong River and Jurong Lake Gardens. Commemorating World Water Day under the theme "Clean Waters, Bright Future," participants engage in a structured two-hour cleanup, documenting findings in journals. The event incorporates SG60 elements — reflection on Singapore's environmental journey, inclusion of diverse participants, meaningful cross-demographic connections, and opportunities for community contribution.</div>
