<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">Our Volleyball Senior Team took part in the Volleyball National School Games, where they showcased their skills, teamwork, and love for the sport. Aligned with the spirit of resilience and sportsmanship, our boys' and girls' teams competed fiercely, demonstrating determination and strong team spirit. Both teams achieved a proud Top 4 finish, earning recognition for their hard work and leadership. 
<br><br>
Throughout the competition, students forged strong bonds, supported one another, and learned valuable lessons about perseverance, unity, and giving their best. It was a memorable experience that celebrated not just victories, but the true spirit of the game!</div><br>
