<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">To welcome the Year of the Fire Horse, our school celebrated Chinese New Year on 16 February with much joy and excitement. The day began with students engaging in various CNY-themed craft activities in their classrooms, such as Paper Horse Making, Sustainable Music Making, and Sustainable Orange Basket Making.
<br><br>
The celebration continued in the hall, where students enjoyed vibrant performances by our CCA groups, PSG, and an energetic Lion Dance. The programme concluded on a high note with a mass singing segment, where students proudly performed using their very own “green” musical instruments, adding a meaningful and eco-friendly touch to the festivities.</div>
