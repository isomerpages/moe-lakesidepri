<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">On 30 March, our school hall was transformed into a vibrant celebration of culture and community for our annual Hari Raya Concert. Beyond celebrating Hari Raya Aidilfitri, the event served as a beautiful bridge for students to appreciate the richness of Malay heritage within our multicultural society.
<br><br>
The event featured kompang and silat, a Hari Raya video, choral speaking, a slideshow and quiz, a dress-up challenge, a fashion showcase, Malay dance, songs and finale. 
<br><br>
A key highlight was the strong participation of students from various levels and backgrounds coming together to make the event a success. Feedback has been overwhelmingly positive, with students raving about the interactive segments and teachers praising the incredible leadership and involvement shown by the student body.</div>
