<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">The National School Games Opening Ceremony was held during PAVE on Friday, 27 February 2026 to celebrate the contributions of student-athletes towards sporting excellence.
<br><br>
Volleyball Captain Jayden Teu of 6 Respect led the Athlete’s Oath, while Clement Boo of 6 Perseverance led the school in reciting the Spectators’ Creed, affirming commitment to fair play and positive support.
<br><br>
Key reminders were shared on discipline, perseverance, and respectful sportsmanship, setting a strong and united tone for the upcoming NSG season.</div>
