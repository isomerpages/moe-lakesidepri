<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">Our Primary 1 students were swept into a lively Ants Exploration programme designed to spark curiosity about the natural world. Through close-up observation and fun, interactive questioning, they uncovered how ant colonies function, the role of the queen ant, teamwork among ants, their diet, and how to identify different types. Highlights included an entertaining magic segment and an up-close look at real ant colonies. A teacher shared that the session was “highly engaging and full of wonder,” with students captivated throughout.</div>
