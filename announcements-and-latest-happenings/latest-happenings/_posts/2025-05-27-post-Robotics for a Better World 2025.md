<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">Our P5 prefects took part in the biannual Robotics for a Better World 2025 organised by Rulang Primary School. The event provided a platform for participants to showcase their creativity, innovative thinking, and problem-solving skills, and encourages them to apply their knowledge to real-world challenges using Design Thinking.
<br><br>
To showcase their ideas, students presented a display board featuring their problem statements and suggested solutions, alongside a physical prototype to a panel of judges.</div>
