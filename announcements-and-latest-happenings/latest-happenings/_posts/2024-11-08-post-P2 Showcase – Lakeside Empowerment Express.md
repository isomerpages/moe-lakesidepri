The Lakeside Empowerment Express has completed its journey! Our Primary 2 pupils captivated audiences with a vibrant showcase of songs, dances, and skits, demonstrating remarkable creativity and teamwork. These diverse performances reinforced our school values and cultivated essential learning dispositions. This journey of active participation empowered pupils to take charge of their learning and discovering the joy of making a positive difference to the themselves and others around them.
