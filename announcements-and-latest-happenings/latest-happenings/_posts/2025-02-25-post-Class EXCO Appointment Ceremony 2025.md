<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">The Class EXCO Appointment Ceremony held within the classroom setting this year emphasized the importance of Leading with L-PRIDE. <br><br>
With their new roles, the appointed EXCO are now equipped to contribute positively to a caring and enabling learning environment.</div>
