<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">Members of the Community Leadership Club embarked on an enriching learning journey to City Square Mall and the CDL Ecotrain, where they explored the importance of environmental sustainability. Through guided tours and hands-on experiences, students witnessed firsthand how sustainable practices are integrated into everyday activities, from eco-friendly building designs to green initiatives championed by the organisations.
<br><br>
The journey deepened their awareness of environmental issues and inspired them to take ownership as community leaders in championing sustainability. Students returned with a renewed sense of purpose and commitment to making a positive environmental impact in their communities.</div>
