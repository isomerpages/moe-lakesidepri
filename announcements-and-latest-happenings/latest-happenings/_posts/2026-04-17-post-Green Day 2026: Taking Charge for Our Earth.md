<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">On Monday, 20 April, in celebration of Earth Day, our school held Green Day 2026 with engaging activities focused on climate change and energy sustainability.
<br><br>
The day began with an assembly programme that highlighted the impact of human actions on the Earth and the importance of taking responsibility for a sustainable future.
<br><br>
During recess, students explored the NEA Climate Change Exhibition, participated in hands-on activities, and completed a quiz to deepen their understanding of environmental issues and actions they can take in their daily lives.
<br><br>
The learning continued beyond the day through Green Day SLS activities, reinforcing key concepts on energy use and sustainability.Green Day 2026 provided a meaningful platform for students to learn, reflect, and take action towards caring for the environment, as we continue to nurture environmentally responsible individuals.
