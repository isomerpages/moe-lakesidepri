<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify"><b>Start Right, End B’Right (SREB)</b> is a special programme designed to help students ease back into school and begin the year with confidence and positivity. Held on 5 and 6 January, SREB focuses on building strong relationships, setting clear directions, and helping students understand expectations for the year ahead. 
<br><br>
Through interactive activities and bonding sessions, students are encouraged to take charge of their learning and make a positive difference in their own ways. By starting the year right, we aim to create a smooth transition, a strong sense of belonging, and a bright, successful year for every child.</div>
