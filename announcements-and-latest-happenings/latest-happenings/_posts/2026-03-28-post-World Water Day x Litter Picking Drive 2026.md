<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">On 28 March 2026, our school community partnered with PacificLight for World Water Day under the theme "Be a Water Hero – Every Action Counts!" at Jurong Lake Gardens. 
<br><br>
School leaders, staff, parent support group members with their children, and PacificLight representatives united to protect our waterways through environmental action. Participants were divided into four groups for systematic litter picking across designated areas, learning about the 4Rs and proper waste segregation. The morning concluded with wrap-up activities at Cascade Pavilion, reinforcing water conservation messages. 
<br><br>
This successful partnership demonstrated how collaborative efforts between schools and corporate supporters create meaningful environmental impact.</div>
