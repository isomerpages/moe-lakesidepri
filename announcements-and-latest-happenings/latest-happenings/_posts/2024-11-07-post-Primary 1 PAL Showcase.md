The Primary 1 Showcase, themed "The World Around Us," was a celebration of creativity and learning where students presented a short performance, which included music, storytelling, or dance. These captivating displays took place in the classroom, offering families a glimpse into their children's learning journey. The showcase concluded with a meaningful family bonding activity, fostering connection and shared joy among students, parents, and teachers.
