<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">Lakeside Primary School's Awards Day 2024, held on 14 February 2025, celebrated student excellence in academics, arts, sports, and character development. Guest of Honour, Ms Jorina Choy, joined in recognizing the outstanding achievements of our students.
<br><br>
Under the theme "Celebrating Achievements, Unleashing Talents," the event showcased the diverse talents within our community. The day fostered a strong sense of pride and accomplishment, inspiring students to strive for even greater heights. It reinforced our school's LPRIDE values and motivated the entire Lakeside family to embrace a culture of excellence. Congratulations to all award recipients!</div>
