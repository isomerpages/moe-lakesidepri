<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">The Primary Two Prata Discovery Experience, conducted on 14 and 15 May, was a meaningful extension of the STELLAR unit on roti prata. Students engaged in a hands-on, authentic learning experience where they explored the origins and cultural significance of this local delicacy. 
<br><br>
Our students observed traditional preparation methods and participated in prata-making activities. This immersive experience allowed students to apply their learning in a real-world context. Through the process, they developed a deeper appreciation of local culture, experienced a strong sense of accomplishment, and enhanced their understanding, making learning both enjoyable and impactful for all.</div>
