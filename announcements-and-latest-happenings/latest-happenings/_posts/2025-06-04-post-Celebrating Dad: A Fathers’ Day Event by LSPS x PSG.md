<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">As part of our Father’s Day celebrations, Lakeside Primary collaborated with the Parent Support Group (PSG) to organise <i>Celebrating Dad</i>—a meaningful bonding event for fathers and their children. The main highlight was an <b>Amazing Race</b> around the school, where each father-child team tackled five exciting challenges: <i>The Sole Search, Pop Goes The Puzzle, Hopstacle Course, The Mindfield, and Who’s The Boss?</i>. The event ended on a heartwarming note with a bonus activity, <i>Spread The Love</i>.
<br><br>
This special event aimed to honour fathers, encourage active involvement in their children’s educational journey, and promote teamwork, communication, and problem-solving. It also served to strengthen the sense of PSG community among families through fun and shared experiences.</div>
