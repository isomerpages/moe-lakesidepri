<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">P6 students participated in an enrichment lesson at The Ecogarden, located at the Singapore Science Centre, exploring the diverse organisms in a pond. 
<br><br>
The Ecogarden, a living outdoor laboratory, offers a wide variety of plants and animals for observation. Students collected different organisms from the pond and brought them back to the laboratory, where they examined and identified them under a microscope. 
<br><br>
Through this hands-on inquiry experience, students developed adaptive thinking by applying their knowledge of biodiversity to real-world contexts. They collaborated in small groups to observe, classify, and identify organisms, strengthening communication and collaboration skills.</div>
