<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">Lakeside Primary School hosted 28 educators from Himachal Pradesh, India, on 26 February 2025 through a collaboration with The Principals Academy. The visit featured classroom observations, discussions on pedagogical practices and cultural exchange. This initiative aimed to foster international educational partnerships and share innovative teaching strategies, reinforcing mutual goals of enhancing student-centered learning experiences.<div>
