<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">The P5 Outdoor Adventure Camp which concluded recently is a platform which provided an exceptional opportunity for our students to refine their 21st Century Competencies while fostering mutual appreciation through teamwork. Engaging in a variety of team-building and outdoor activities, students not only had the chance to embody and strengthen our core school values of perseverance and respect but also experienced the challenges and vibrancy of nature. This dynamic setting encouraged personal growth and a deeper connection with both peers and the environment.</div>
