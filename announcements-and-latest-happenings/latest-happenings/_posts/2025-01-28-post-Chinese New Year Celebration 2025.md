<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">Chinese New Year holds a special place in the hearts of Singaporeans, and this year, our entire Lakers community came together to celebrate in style. Students enjoyed a wide range of engaging activities in class, and the auditorium buzzed with excitement as our CCA groups and PSG presented dazzling performances.<br><br>
We were also honored to welcome seniors from the Lions Charity, whose presence enriched our celebration and helped foster a spirit of care, respect, and giving among our students.<br><br>
Wishing everyone a Happy New Year and a prosperous Year of the Snake!</div>
