<div style="font-size:14pt;" align="justify">Our Lakers participated in a meaningful online cultural exchange with students from Anubansamsen School and Wat Bot School. The two schools are from Thailand, Bangkok.
<br><br>
During the sessions, they explored the cultural aspects of Singapore and Thailand, which helped to build greater cross-cultural understanding and appreciation. 
<br><br>
The students also discussed environmental sustainability efforts in the two countries and considered ways they could play a part in adopting more sustainable practices.</div>
